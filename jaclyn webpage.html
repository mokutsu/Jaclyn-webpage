My running story.

I started running when I was very young and competitively when I was 15. I was competitive all throughout highschool and took a bit of a break while in University. I ran a little bit recreationally but it wasnt until I was 26 that I really picked it back up (8 years since graduating highschool!) Since then I have ran the New York, London, Berlin and Chicago Marathons, completed seven half Marathons and a handful of 5 km and 10 km races. I most recently ran my 5 km and half marathon pbs!
